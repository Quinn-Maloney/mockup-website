This Lifeguard Conference is designed to teach participants the knowledge and skills necessary to prevent
and respond to aquatic emergencies. The course content will prepare participants to recognize and respond
quickly and effectively to emergencies as well as prevent drownings and injuries at facilities. All candidates
must be at least 16 years of age at the start of the session.

Conference Date:

July 20th, 2021

The main roles of a lifeguard are to prevent accidents by spotting potential hazards before they
become life threatening, and to respond to emergencies when they occur.

Additionally, they also educate the public about the potential risks associated in aquatic facilities
and how to prevent these risks and be safe during aquatic activities.
