The main roles of a lifeguard are to prevent accidents by spotting potential hazards before they become life
threatening, and to respond to emergencies when they occur.

Additionally, they also educate the public about the potential risks associated in aquatic facilities
and how to prevent these risks and be safe during aquatic activities.
